### The Holy Grail of Drug Design

Predicting the binding affinity ($$\Delta G_{bind}$$) is crucial for optimizing drug leads.

### Methods

- **MM/PBSA**: A popular end-point method that balances accuracy and computational cost.
- **FEP (Free Energy Perturbation)**: A rigorous alchemical method that is more accurate but computationally expensive.

Binding free energy calculations help rank ligands and guide chemical modifications to improve potency.
