Welcome to My Website!

Who am I?

My name is Konstantin, and I'm a proud graduate of the University of Bristol, where I achieved a First Class Honours degree in Chemistry with Computing. Having recently moved to the United States, I am excited to dive into new opportunities and challenges that align with my passion for merging the worlds of computing and chemistry.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a deep interest in how data science, software engineering, and high-performance computing (HPC) can drive scientific progress. I am deeply grateful that my University course allowed me to explore the powerful contributions that computational chemistry can make research.

What can you find here?

  • It's a place for me to showcase my work and experience.
  • It's a place for me to share my thoughts and ideas.
  • It's a place for you to learn more about me and get in touch.

I also hope to be able to put up little programming guides to teach you about jekyll, css, and html as I build and expand the website. There is a chance that this will turn into broader programming guides for beginners, but we'll see how it goes.
